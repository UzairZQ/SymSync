What SymSync does

No more chaos. Just clarity between left and right.

Surface EMG is noisy, sensitive, and notoriously hard to interpret. SymSync does the hard part — synchronizing two channels, filtering noise, and surfacing a single, trustworthy symmetry index in real time.

Live bilateral monitoring

Two biosignalsplux sensors stream simultaneously over Bluetooth. SymSync pairs them, aligns the timestamps, and plots a live symmetry index you can act on — not raw voltage noise.

How it works

From sensor to insight in three steps.

A typical session takes less than 90 seconds to set up, runs in real time on your phone, and gives you a number you can actually trust.

01

Pair over Bluetooth

Open the app, hit connect. SymSync discovers your biosignalsplux hub, syncs both channels, and confirms the connection — all permission handling included.

02

Calibrate & move

Hold a neutral posture for two seconds. The app learns your baseline. Now run stairs, squats, gait drills — whatever the protocol calls for.

03

Read the result

Watch the symmetry index climb, the heatmap update, and the history chart fill in. End the session — your summary is saved automatically.

Imbalance heatmap

See which side is carrying the load.

The heatmap renders a 4×14 grid of intensity dots over each leg's thigh and calf, mapped to the underlying activation. Cool dots mean low effort; hot dots mean high effort. The asymmetry your eye can't catch, the heatmap can't hide.

  • Intensity-mapped colour scale from cool to hot
  • Synchronous overlay on anatomical leg diagram
  • Live updates at 20Hz alongside the waveform chart
Dashboard

One number that means something.

SymSync's home screen shows a single symmetry index — the inverse of your left/right deviation, normalised. Above 90 means balanced. Below 75 means one side is doing significantly more work. The radial chart updates live during a session.

  • Channel A / Channel B raw EMG with peak indicators
  • Average symmetry and best balance across your history
  • One-tap connect / disconnect for the biosignalsplux hub

Built for

One app, three very different jobs.

SymSync was designed around the same question — is this side working as hard as the other? — and how that question changes depending on whether you're training, recovering, or assessing.

Athlete

Train balanced, not just hard.

Stair climbing, squats, single-leg plyometrics. Watch the index climb session by session as you correct dominant-side compensation.

Rehabilitation

Track recovery between visits.

Quantify how the affected side is catching up. Share the trend chart with your physiotherapist before your next appointment.

Stair protocol

The classic bilateral stress test.

SymSync's default 60-second stair protocol surfaces asymmetry in one of the most demanding, most common rehab exercises.

Clinician

A second pair of eyes between sessions.

Patients self-record at home. You see the deviation, balance, and trend on their profile — not just how they moved in front of you.

The science

Built on instruments clinicians already trust.

SymSync reads from biosignalsplux — research-grade surface EMG hardware used in human-movement labs worldwide. The mobile app is the missing layer: the one that turns the signal into a number you can coach against.

Research-grade sensors

biosignalsplux is the standard in academic EMG research. Same hardware, same signal quality — wrapped in a phone UI.

Transparent signal chain

Calibration, baseline, rectification, RMS — every step of the pipeline is documented and inspectable, not a black box.

Your data, your device

All session history is stored locally by default. No cloud, no account, no telemetry. Export, sync, and share only when you choose to.
